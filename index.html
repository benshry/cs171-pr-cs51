Introduction

"CS51: Factors Affecting Student Success" is a project investigating the performance of students in Computer Science 51, an introductory CS course at Harvard. It examines the relationship between a student's grade on assignments and their year, initial level of programming comfort, and interaction with the course's online discussion forum.
